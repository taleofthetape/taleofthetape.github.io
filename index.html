Data Source and Game Information

**The Data:** All fighter statistics and ranking information are scraped nightly at midnight EST from the official **UFC Rankings** page and individual **UFC Athlete Profile** pages using a custom Python script. This ensures the game is based on the most up-to-date data available.

**How Comparison Works:**

  • **Green:** Exact match in value or category (Division, Rank).
  • **Yellow:** The statistic is *close* to the secret fighter's value (within 10-50% difference).
  • **Red:** The statistic is far off (more than 50% difference).
  • **Arrows (▲ or ▼):** The arrow indicates the direction the secret fighter's stat lies. A **▼** means your guessed stat is too **High**, and the secret fighter's stat is **Lower**. A **▲** means your guessed stat is too **Low**, and the secret fighter's stat is **Higher**.
