Operation Briefing

  • **Data Source:** All statistical data is scraped nightly from the official **UFC.com** rankings and athlete pages, ensuring daily accuracy.
  • **Data Update:** The data is pulled by a GitHub Action that runs every night at **midnight EST**. If the game seems stuck, the data might be updating.
  • **Comparison Guide:**
    • **GREEN:** Exact match or very close (less than 10% difference).
    • **YELLOW/GOLD:** Close proximity (10% to 50% difference).
    • **RED:** Significant deviation (more than 50% difference).
  • **Arrow Guide (Unicode):**
    • **↓ (DOWN):** Your guessed stat is **Higher** than the secret fighter's stat. The secret fighter is **LOWER**.
    • **↑ (UP):** Your guessed stat is **Lower** than the secret fighter's stat. The secret fighter is **HIGHER**.
